VISHWAKARMA-UNITY CENTER OF EXCELLENCE (VUCOE)

To create a skilled pool of human resources in Immersive Technologies.

Immersive technologies are finding applications in important areas like education, training, medical, healthcare, film making, cinematography, animation and gaming, smart manufacturing (Industry 4.0), automotive and transportation, architecture, art and design, museums etc

Vishwakarma University in collaboration with “Unity”, world’s leading cross platform real-time engine, sets up a Centre of Excellence in Immersive Technologies (Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, and Mixed Reality) & Tools, at Vishwakarma University Campus. This unique centre proposes to offer an interactive design and development experience (2D / 3D) in varied domains viz. engineering, art and design, architecture, film-making, entertainment, psychology, management, travel and tourism through simulations and visualizations

VISION

To be the Global Centre of Excellence for Training, Development and Research in Immersive Technologies (AR/VR/MR)

MISSION

  • Creating creative, competent and valuable pool of skilled human resources in Immersive Technologies (IT)
  • Providing quality professional services of training/development in IT to industries/individuals around globe.
  • Building a world-class experience center in Immersive Technologies
  • Empowering the skilled resources in maximizing their career opportunities and academic pursuits in IT.
  • Making effective use of IT for solving problems in education, healthcare and other sectors etc.. having social relevance and national interest.
  • Providing eco-system for incubating start-ups in IT

    • UNITY STUDENT CLUB

    The Unity Student Club, coordinated by Dr. Yogesh Deshpande, comprises of both students and staff from in and out of the university, where they are trained on Unity platform. The students are divided into batches according to their academic timetables. Each batch is provided with a verified instructor. Students try to build various projects along with their instructors. Various events are held like the Unity Day and Unite conferences every year where students get a chance to showcase their projects in front of a huge audience. Students are consulted and also get a chance to apply for various internships. The students of the club also participate in hackathons, certification exams and Unity game developers meets. Students are also facilitated with Experience Zone and Development Lab, where they can explore various VR/AR tech as well as carry out their projects and research work
