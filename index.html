VISHWAKARMA-UNITY CENTER OF EXCELLENCE

Vishwakarma University in collaboration with “Unity”, world’s leading cross platform real-time engine,
sets up a Centre of Excellence in Immersive Technologies (Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, and Mixed Reality) & Tools, at Vishwakarma University Campus.
‘Unity’ is used to create half of the world’s video games. It is also used for creating non-gaming mixed reality immersive experiences in various domains.
“Vishwakarma-Unity Center of Excellence” will pioneer in training, development and research in Immersive Technologies (AR/VR/MR).
The aim of the centre is to create a skilled pool of human resources in Immersive Technologies.
